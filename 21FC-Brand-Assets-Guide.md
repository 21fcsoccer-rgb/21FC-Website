## Logo Variations Inventory

### Logo Types

1. **Full Logo (with Baller/Player Silhouette)** — The primary mark. "21FC" text with a soccer player doing an overhead/bicycle kick integrated between the "1" and "F". This is the hero logo used on the Instagram profile picture.

2. **Court Baller Logo** — Vertical stack version with the full player silhouette prominently displayed. Great for square formats, merchandise, and hero sections.

3. **Text Logo (One Color)** — Just the "21FC" typography without the player silhouette. Clean, versatile, works at small sizes. Good for navigation bars and footers.

4. **Alternate 1** — Modified version of the main logo. Available in multiple colorways.

5. **Alternate 2** — Second alternate design. Available in multiple colorways.

6. **Baller Vertical Stack** — Vertical arrangement with player silhouette. Works great for tall/portrait formats.

7. **Text Vertical Stack** — Vertical text arrangement without the player. Clean and stackable.

## Photo Shoot: 21FC Exports March 26

**Google Drive Folder:** [21FC Exports March 26](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1tulxjAU3c5MU9FhW2ZoEagjIK3lrdlGA)
**Photographer:** Palao Design Studio
**Date:** March 19-20, 2026
**Files:** 15+ high-resolution PNGs (2-21 MB each, DSC camera exports)

### What's in the Shoot

These are professionally shot and edited marketing photos — not raw game footage. Every image has branded 21FC graphic overlays built in. The set includes:

**Player Portraits (Vertical/Portrait Format):**
- Individual player portraits with branded 21FC backgrounds
- Players in blue 21FC bibs and red 21FC kits
- Soccer field diagram overlays with logo branding
- Multiple color themes per portrait: pink/red, blue/navy, gold/yellow, grey/white

**Action Shots (Both Portrait and Landscape):**
- Two-player competitive matchups (red vs blue kit)
- Dynamic ball-control action with motion blur effects
- Multiple branded backgrounds: deep blue neon, green, gold, grey/white

**Color Themes Across the Shoot:**
- Blue/Navy neon (dramatic, nightclub energy)
- Green (vibrant, energy-forward)
- Gold/Yellow (warm, premium feel)
- Grey/White (clean, editorial)
- Pink/Red with soccer field lines (signature 21FC energy)

### Recommended Website Placements

| Photo Type | Best Placement | Recommended File(s) |
|------------|---------------|---------------------|
| Landscape action (green or blue) | **Hero background** | DSC08674.png or DSC08619.png |
| Portrait player shot (blue neon) | **About section / Player spotlight** | DSC08800.png or DSC08703.png |
| Gold/yellow action shot | **Schedule section accent** | DSC08820.png |
| Red kit player portrait | **Gallery featured card** | DSC08712.png or DSC08641.png |
| Grey/white wide action | **CTA banner background** | DSC08730.png |

### Technical Notes
- All files are high-res PNGs (great quality but large — will need compression/resizing for web)
- Recommend resizing to max 1920px wide and converting to WebP for website use
- The branded overlays are baked into the images — no need to add logos separately
- These photos already match the website's dark aesthetic perfectly
