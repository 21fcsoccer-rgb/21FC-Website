# 21FC Soccer Club — Website Site Plan

## Brand Overview

**Club Name:** 21FC
**Tagline:** Members Only
**Location:** Clifton, NJ
**What they do:** Adult pickup soccer — community-driven, competitive, members-only
**Schedule:** 7AM games on Mon, Wed, Fri & Sun (indoor turf)
**Instagram:** @21fc.soccer (3,400+ followers, 114 posts, verified)

---

## Brand Identity (Pulled from Instagram)

**Logo:** Stylized gothic/street-art "21FC" text with a soccer player silhouette kicking a ball. Versatile — appears in multiple colorways (white, black, blue, red, pink).

**Primary Colors:**
- Hot Pink / Magenta (#E91E8C) — profile picture, standout brand color
- Deep Navy / Dark Blue (#0A1628) — dominant background, overlays
- White (#FFFFFF) — text and logo on dark backgrounds

**Accent Colors:**
- Electric Blue (#3B5EF5)
- Red (#D42A2A)
- Neon Green/Yellow (#CCFF00) — pinnies/training bibs
- Black (#000000)

**Typography Vibe:** Bold, gothic-influenced. Think Oswald, Bebas Neue, or similar condensed/impactful fonts paired with a clean sans-serif for body text.

**Overall Aesthetic:** Dark mode, moody, streetwear-inspired. Indoor soccer under lights. More "soccer lifestyle brand" than traditional rec league. Young, energetic, community-focused.

## Site Structure (5 Pages)

### 1. HOME (Landing Page)
**Purpose:** Hook visitors, communicate the vibe, drive sign-ups

**Sections:**
- **Hero** — Full-width dark background with the 21FC logo, tagline "Members Only," and a bold CTA: "Join the Club" (links to OpenSports)
- **What is 21FC?** — Short blurb: adult pickup soccer, Clifton NJ, 7AM games, competitive but community-driven
- **Schedule at a Glance** — Visual calendar strip showing Mon/Wed/Fri/Sun game days and times
- **Photo/Video Reel** — Auto-scrolling gallery of action shots and team photos from Instagram
- **Social Proof** — Follower count, member testimonials, "3,400+ on Instagram"
- **CTA Banner** — "Ready to play? Sign up on OpenSports" with button

### 2. ABOUT
**Purpose:** Tell the 21FC story, introduce the founder

**Sections:**
- **Our Story** — How 21FC started, the mission, the "Members Only" philosophy
- **Meet the Founder** — Photo + short bio (the owner featured in their Instagram content)
- **The Community** — What makes 21FC different: diverse players, former college athletes (Felician University shoutout), co-ed, all skill levels welcome within competitive play
- **Partners & Sponsors** — Logos for Parabolic, PBC, Daza, and any other partners

### 3. SCHEDULE & JOIN
**Purpose:** Show game schedule and drive OpenSports sign-ups

**Sections:**
- **Weekly Schedule** — Clean table/grid: Day | Time | Location | Spots Available
- **OpenSports Integration** — Embedded link or iframe to OpenSports app for registration. Primary CTA: "Reserve Your Spot" button linking directly to their OpenSports page
- **Membership Info** — How the members-only model works: pricing tiers, drop-in vs. membership, what's included
- **FAQ** — Common questions (skill level needed, what to bring, cancellation policy, etc.)

### 4. GALLERY
**Purpose:** Showcase the culture and energy

**Sections:**
- **Photo Grid** — Curated selection of best Instagram photos (action shots, team photos, player spotlights)
- **Instagram Feed Embed** — Live feed from @21fc.soccer so it stays fresh without manual updates
- **Player Spotlights** — Featured player cards (FIFA-card style, matching their Instagram aesthetic)

### 5. CONTACT
**Purpose:** Make it easy to reach 21FC

**Sections:**
- **Contact Form** — Name, email, message (simple)
- **Social Links** — Instagram (primary), plus any others
- **Location Info** — Clifton, NJ with embedded Google Map of the facility
- **DM on Instagram** — Direct link to message @21fc.soccer

## Design Direction

**Style:** Dark mode by default. Moody, bold, high-contrast. Think Nike Football meets streetwear brand site.

**Layout:** Full-width sections, big imagery, minimal clutter. Mobile-first (most of their audience likely finds them via Instagram on their phones).

**Fonts (Suggested):**
- Headlines: **Oswald** or **Bebas Neue** (bold, condensed — matches the gothic logo energy)
- Body: **Inter** or **Montserrat** (clean, modern, highly readable)

**Imagery:** Dark backgrounds with bright accent pops. Indoor turf under lights. Action photography. Player portraits with dramatic lighting.

**Animations:** Subtle fade-ins on scroll. Hover effects on buttons and cards. Nothing over the top — let the photography do the talking.
